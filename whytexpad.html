$\text{Why TexPad?}$

Return

You may be similar to me in that sometimes it's just too much work to open your inventory, reach into it, and acquire a notebook and pencil. This website offers a unique solution by providing a versatile framework for the user to freely input and manipulate mathematical expressions typed in LaTeX. This utility, however, should not solely be associated with a solution for laziness; indeed, it is a practical tool as there exist many instances where writing simply isn't feasible.


This version of TexPad is the first release: v0.1. This is, perhaps, just 5% of the final product. The following is a list of soon-to-be implementations:
  • 2D and 3D Desmos Integration
  • Wolfram Integration
  • Background Customizability
  • Drawing/Sketching Tool, Compass, Straightedge, Bezier
  • Free-Move, Drag and Drop
  • Individual Resizability
  • Saving and Loading
  • And more!
